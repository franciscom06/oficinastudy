Project Sitestar - OFICINA, Escola Profissional

What is this project?

We were challenged to take part in a programming competition, where the aim was to create an innovative project that linked technology and education. With this in mind, we decided to develop an Artificial Intelligence dedicated to studies, capable of providing answers with greater precision and helping students to learn more efficiently. Our AI was designed to understand questions in various areas of knowledge, analyze the information available in a file, and thus provide accurate and relevant answers to students' questions. Unlike other AIs, ours gives any teacher the opportunity to add knowledge to it just by typing it into a text file.

The AI and platform are at a very early stage, both in terms of knowledge and functions. Here's just a demonstration of the AI and what it can do.
